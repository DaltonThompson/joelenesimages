FAQ

How long are normal sessions?
About one hour, but if we go over, it's okay! I break up my clients into 2 hour time periods, so if the baby is crying there is time to feed and change.

My child may find it hard to adjust to visiting a new studio. How can you you help?
Because I schedule extra time for sessions, we can take some time to play so they are more comfortable in the studio. I also give special attention to children with disabilities

Can I do on-site photo shoots?
Yes! I can suggest magnificent scenery, or we can do a shoot at your home. Pets are always welcome at the studio or outside, whichever you prefer. McDougall & Piper are 2 of my favorite 4 legged friends.

What will it be like if you come to my wedding?
We start from the beginning of the day until the Bride and Groom have left the venue. Our clients don't need to stress over staged cake-cutting photos, unless they really want to! We love to capture the beautiful moments that are hard to imitate, so you can get straight to making wonderful memories for your new lives together.
